About TextCanvas

TextCanvas is an advanced image-to-text conversion engine built in Python by Zachory Pelletier.

Core Tools:
  • IMG to Text Generator: A powerful single-image processor with real-time preview and fine-tuning controls.
  • IMG to Text Batch Generator: A bulk processing tool allowing you to convert up to 10 images or URLs simultaneously with individual management.

It utilizes a unique "Subpixel" mode that uses the Upper Half Block character to double vertical resolution, preserving the true colors of your original image in text form.
