Instructions

Follow these steps to use TextCanvas:

  • Select Source: Toggle between Device or URL sources. Device source is strictly Drag & Drop.
  • Managing State: Use the Reset button to revert image settings, or Delete Image (or images) to remove image files entirely.
  • Output Styles: Experiment with LCD Subpixel for high fidelity, or Matrix and ASCII for classic looks.
  • Rate Limits: To ensure performance, PNG downloads are limited to 5 every minute.
  • Save: Download your creations as PNG, Zip, or Text.
