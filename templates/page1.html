Instructions

Follow these steps to use TextCanvas:

  • 1. Select Source: Toggle between Device IMG Source or URL IMG Source. Device mode uses strict Drag & Drop.
  • 2. IMG to Text Generator: Perfect for single images. Use the sliders to adjust contrast and brightness live.
  • 3. Batch Generator: Drag up to 10 images at once. Use the red × to remove specific images or the Download icon ⬇ to save them individually.
  • 4. Output Styles: Experiment with LCD Subpixel (True Color), Matrix Rain, ASCII, and Dithering modes.
  • 5. Save: Download your creations as PNG or Copy the raw text to your clipboard.
